The Artist

Built Different

PATE doesn't fit a box — never tried to. By day he's grinding like everyone else. By night, he's in the studio stacking 808s and laying vocals that sound like they rolled straight off a dirt road at midnight.

"Country grit, city bass. Truck culture with a trap soul. That's Houston in two lines."

Born and built in Houston, Texas — a city that never apologized for doing things its own way. PATE's music lives in that same unapologetic space. Hick-hop isn't a genre to him, it's a lifestyle he was already living before he had a name for it.

No label. No co-sign needed. Just real music for people who know the feeling of a cold beer, a hot beat, and a truck with the bass cranked all the way up.
