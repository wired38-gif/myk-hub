About the Artist

No templates.
No AI filler.

"I make art because I have to. The culture demands it."

JMichael is an independent creative out of Houston, TX — the kind of artist that doesn't fit neatly into one box. Design, apparel, music merch, brand work. If it needs to look like something real, that's the work.

LTI started as a way to put everything under one roof: the Inspire® streetwear line, digital art commissions, music merch for independent artists, and custom brand kits for artists and small businesses who deserve better than generic.

ADHD-driven creativity is a feature, not a flaw. It means every project gets obsessive attention to the details that matter — composition, color theory, cultural context. The work feels alive because it is.

How It Works

From brief to
delivered.

No surprise revisions, no scope creep, no ghosting. A clear four-step process keeps the work moving and the communication open from day one.

Step 01
Brief
Fill out the commission form with your project details — what you need, your budget, your vibe, and your timeline. The more detail, the better the result.
Step 02
Concept
I develop initial concepts and share them for feedback. We align on direction before the final build starts — no wasted effort on either side.
Step 03
Build
The real work. Full execution with revisions built into the process. You'll see the work as it comes together, not just at the final delivery.
Step 04
Deliver
Final files in every format you need — print-ready, web-ready, apparel-ready. Delivered clean, organized, and ready to use from day one.
